DrupalCamp Charlotte

Charlotte, North Carolina • June 10th, 2012

Hey there, welcome to the home of the inaugural DrupalCamp Charlotte. This camp is put on by the good folks at the Charlotte Drupal Users Group (CharDUG), a collection of enthusiastic individuals smitten with Drupal. Sunday, June 10, 2012 will feature a bevy of breakout sessions, birds of a feather (BoF) groups, and a keynote speech at the Blake Hotel in Charlotte's Uptown neighborhood. Our sessions and groups will have a little something for everyone, catering to everyone from beginning content editors to advanced developers.

Make sure to mark June 10th, 2012 on your calendar and check back in March for registration details.
